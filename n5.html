🏞️ Thoonakkadavu Dam

📍 Location: Inside Parambikulam Tiger Reserve, about 90 km from Coimbatore
🏗️ Engineering Marvel – Built across the Parambikulam River, contributing to irrigation & hydroelectricity 💡


🌟 Why Visit Thoonakkadavu Dam?
✅ Scenic Beauty – Surrounded by lush green forests & rolling hills 🌳🏔️
✅ Boating Bliss – Glide across calm waters with stunning reflections of nature 🚣‍♂️
✅ Wildlife Paradise – Spot elephants, tigers, gaurs, leopards & 250+ bird species 🐅🦜
✅ Offbeat & Peaceful – Less crowded than Topslip, ideal for nature lovers 🤫💚
✅ Historical Landmark – Home to the 450-year-old Kannimara Teak Tree 🌲

🏕️ Things to Do
🌿 Guided Safaris – Explore deep forests with expert forest guides 🚙
🚶‍♂️ Nature Walks – Trek through untouched trails for a true wilderness experience 🥾
🏡 Eco-Stays – Stay in forest lodges & treehouses for an immersive jungle vibe 🌲

🗓️ Best Time to Visit
🍃 October - March – Cool weather & frequent wildlife sightings make it the best season!


🌊 Thoonakkadavu Dam is a hidden paradise, perfect for an offbeat adventure!
