Black Thunder Water Theme Park

Located in Mettupalayam, 40 km from Coimbatore, Black Thunder is one of the largest water theme parks in India. Spanning over 75 acres, this park features exciting water rides, thrill slides, and adventure activities. Some of the top attractions include: Wave Pool – A massive pool that generates artificial waves for a beach-like experience. Lazy River – A slow-moving stream where visitors can float in tubes and relax. Boomerang Slide – A thrilling water slide where riders experience a free-fall effect. Rain Dance Zone – A fun area with music, disco lights, and water showers. Adventure Rides – Includes roller coasters, go-karting, and a 5D cinema experience. The park also has restaurants, snack bars, and picnic spots, making it a great family-friendly getaway from Coimbatore.
