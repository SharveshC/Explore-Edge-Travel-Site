🏔️ Perumal Peak - A Trekker’s Paradise

📍 Location: Near Kodaikanal, 60 km from Coimbatore
🌄️ One of the highest peaks in the Western Ghats at 2,440 meters (8,005 feet)


🌟 Why Visit Perumal Peak?
✅ Breathtaking Views – Enjoy panoramic views of the Nilgiri Hills & lush Western Ghats
✅ Moderate Trek – Suitable for both beginners & experienced trekkers
✅ Mystical Climate – Cool temperatures with mist-covered trails create a surreal experience
✅ Best Sunrise & Sunset Spot – Witness stunning sky transitions from the peak

🥾 Trekking Details
📍 Starting Point: Perumal Malai Village
⛰️ Trail Type: Well-marked but steep & rocky in sections
🎒 Essentials: Carry water, food & warm clothing (trek is in a reserved forest area)

🌟 Best Time to Visit
🗓️ November - March – Cool & clear visibility for the best trekking experience


If you're an adventure lover, nature enthusiast, or simply someone seeking peaceful mountain vibes, Perumal Peak is a must-visit near Coimbatore!
