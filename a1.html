🙏 Velliangiri Andavar Temple - The Sacred South Kailash

The Velliangiri Andavar Temple, revered as the South Kailash, is located atop the mystical Velliangiri Hills...

🏔️ Sacred Pilgrimage & Trekking Experience:
🔹 Nestled in the Velliangiri Hills, a 14 km barefoot trek through forests and rocky terrain.
🔹 Known as South Kailash, equivalent to a pilgrimage to Mount Kailash.

🌟 Spiritual & Historical Significance:
🔹 Lord Shiva is worshipped as Velliangiri Andavar in the form of a Swayambu Lingam.
🔹 Associated with Goddess Parvati’s penance and meditation by ancient sages.

🎉 Festivals & Celebrations:
🔹 Most auspicious during Maha Shivaratri & Tamil months of Chithirai & Vaikasi (April–June).
🔹 Thousands of devotees undertake the trek chanting 'Om Namah Shivaya'.
🕰️ Temple Experience:

🔹 Pilgrims report mystical encounters, divine visions, and a sense of ultimate peace.

A life-changing journey for spiritual seekers and adventure lovers alike!
