🌊 Vaidehi Falls

📍 Location: 35 km from Coimbatore, in the Boluvampatti forest range 🌲🌄️
🌿 A Hidden Natural Wonder – A perfect escape for nature lovers & adventure seekers


🌟 Why Visit Vaidehi Falls?
✅ Scenic Waterfall – Cascades from a great height into a natural pool 💦
✅ Exciting Trekking Experience – Walk through dense forests & winding trails 🥾🌳
✅ Wildlife Sightings – Spot elephants, leopards & rare birds 🐘🦅
✅ Peaceful & Less Crowded – Perfect for solitude & tranquility 🌿✨
✅ Post-Monsoon Beauty – Best time to visit: October - February 🍃

⚠️ Things to Remember
🔹 Permission Required – Entry is managed by the Tamil Nadu Forest Department
🔹 Swimming Restricted – Due to safety concerns & wildlife presence
🔹 Carry Essentials – Water, snacks, and good trekking shoes recommended 🎢


🏞️ Vaidehi Falls offers the perfect mix of adventure, nature, and relaxation!
