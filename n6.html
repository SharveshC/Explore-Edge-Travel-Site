🌊 Monkey Falls

📍 Location: 30 km from Coimbatore on the Pollachi-Valparai highway
🏩 Nestled in Anamalai Tiger Reserve – Surrounded by dense forests & rocky cliffs 🌳🌄️


🌟 Why Visit Monkey Falls?
✅ Breathtaking Waterfalls – Crystal-clear water plunges from great heights 💦
✅ Refreshing Natural Pool – Enjoy a cool dip in nature's best spa 🏊‍♂️
✅ Wildlife Encounters – Spot monkeys, peacocks, butterflies & rare birds 🐵🥜
✅ Scenic Road Trips – Drive through winding roads & misty hills 🚗🌄️
✅ Perfect for Picnics & Trekking – Explore short trails & viewpoints 🧟‍♂️

🗓️ Best Time to Visit
🍃 September - February – Enjoy the peak water flow & lush green scenery 🌿

🎒 Things to Remember
🔹 Beware of monkeys! 🐒 – Keep food & valuables safe
🔹 Entry managed by the Forest Department – Follow safety rules & regulations
🔹 Nearby Attractions – Visit Aliyar Dam, Valparai & Anamalai Tiger Reserve 🛅


🌄️ Monkey Falls is the perfect getaway for adventure, relaxation & nature lovers!
