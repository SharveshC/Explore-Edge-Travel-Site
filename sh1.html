🏬 Brookefields Mall - Coimbatore’s Shopping Hub

Brookefields Mall is the largest and most premium shopping mall in Coimbatore, located in RS Puram. Opened in 2009, it spans 400,000+ sq. ft., making it one of the top shopping destinations in Tamil Nadu.

🛍️ Shopping & Brands:
🏪 Home to 120+ national & international brands like Levi’s, Adidas, Nike, Lifestyle, Max, Van Heusen.
🎁 A go-to place for fashion, accessories, cosmetics & home décor.


🎬 Entertainment & Leisure:
🍿 PVR Cinemas – Watch the latest blockbusters in a world-class multiplex.
🎮 Fun City – A gaming zone with arcade games, VR experiences & interactive rides.


🍽️ Food Court & Cafés:
🍔 McDonald's, KFC, Pizza Hut – Enjoy your favorite fast food.
☕ Starbucks, Café Coffee Day, Barista – Sip on premium coffee blends.
🍩 Krispy Kreme, The Donut Company – Indulge in sweet treats.


🚗 Facilities & Events:
🅿️ 1,000+ vehicle parking, centralized air-conditioning, & festive events.
🎉 Hosts fashion shows, live music, & seasonal sales throughout the year!


A must-visit for shopping, entertainment, and dining under one roof!
