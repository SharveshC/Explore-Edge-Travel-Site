🌊 Black Thunder - Water Theme Park

Black Thunder, located in Mettupalayam, just 40 km from Coimbatore, is one of the largest water theme parks in India.
🌊 Spanning 75 acres, it offers thrilling water rides, adventure activities, and entertainment for all ages.


Top Attractions: 🌊 Wave Pool – Experience artificial waves just like at the beach.
🏦 Lazy River – Float and relax in a slow-moving water stream.
🎢 Boomerang Slide – A high-speed slide with a free-fall effect.
🎶 Rain Dance Zone – Dance under disco lights & water showers.
🎡 Adventure Rides – Includes roller coasters, go-karting, and 5D cinema.

🏥 Family-Friendly Amenities: 🍽️ Restaurants & Snack Bars
🌳 Picnic Spots & Relaxation Zones
🎁 Souvenir Shops

Whether you're an adventure enthusiast or looking for a relaxing getaway, Black Thunder is the perfect destination near Coimbatore.
