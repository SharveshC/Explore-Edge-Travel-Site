🌿 Coonoor - A Peaceful Retreat in the Nilgiris

🌄️ Coonoor, located 70 km from Coimbatore, is a serene hill station in the Nilgiris, known for its rolling tea plantations, misty valleys, and peaceful vibes. Unlike its crowded neighbor Ooty, Coonoor is less commercialized, making it a perfect escape for nature lovers & adventure seekers.

✨ Top Attractions in Coonoor:
🌳 Sim’s Park – A beautiful botanical garden with rare plant species & colorful flowers.
🌄 Dolphin’s Nose – A breathtaking viewpoint overlooking Catherine Falls & the Coimbatore plains.
☀️ Lamb’s Rock – A scenic sunrise point with stunning landscapes.
💦 Law’s Falls – A picturesque waterfall surrounded by lush greenery.

🍃 Tea & Local Specialties:
🍵 Visit Highfield Tea Estate & Tranquilitea for tea tasting & factory tours.
🍫 Try local chocolates, homemade cheese & eucalyptus oil from charming local shops.
🚂 Nilgiri Mountain Railway (Toy Train) – Experience one of India’s most scenic train journeys through tunnels & forests.

🧦 For Adventure Lovers:
⛰️ Droog Fort Trek – A historic military outpost of Tipu Sultan with panoramic views.
🌿 Hidden Valley – A secluded nature retreat with diverse flora & fauna.
🌸 Annual Fruit & Vegetable Show – A lively event showcasing Nilgiris' agricultural beauty.

Whether you seek adventure, relaxation, or scenic beauty, Coonoor is a hidden paradise waiting to be explored!
