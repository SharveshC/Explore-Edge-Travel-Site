🙏 Eachanari Vinayagar Temple - A Sacred Ganesha Shrine

Eachanari Vinayagar Temple, located about 12 km from Coimbatore, is one of the most revered temples dedicated to Lord Vinayagar (Ganesha)...

🏛️ Historical & Architectural Marvel:
🔹 Dating back to the 16th century, built around a legendary Ganesha idol.
🔹 Features Dravidian-style gopuram and a gold-plated idol.

🌟 Divine Rituals & Offerings:
🔹 Daily abhishekam, poojas, and special ceremonies.
🔹 Facilities for weddings, homams, and anna dhanam.

🎉 Festivals & Celebrations:
🔹 Grand celebrations of Vinayaka Chathurthi, Thaipoosam, Deepavali.
🔹 Devotees partake in spiritual gatherings and processions.

🕰️ Temple Timings:
🔹 Open daily for darshan and special poojas.

A must-visit for devotees seeking success and blessings!
