Brookefields Mall

Brookefields Mall is the largest and most premium shopping mall in Coimbatore, located in RS Puram, a central and upscale area of the city. Opened in 2009, this multi-level shopping complex spans over 400,000 square feet, making it one of the most visited malls in Tamil Nadu. The mall is designed to offer a complete retail, entertainment, and dining experience, making it an ideal destination for families, youngsters, and corporate professionals.
Brookefields features over 120 brands, both Indian and international, including Levi’s, Adidas, Nike, Max, Lifestyle, Van Heusen, FabIndia, and more. Whether you are looking for trendy apparel, premium accessories, cosmetics, or home decor, this mall has a store for everything. The Lifestyle departmental store offers a wide range of branded clothing, footwear, and beauty products, while Home Centre provides elegant furniture and home essentials.
Entertainment is another major highlight of the mall. The PVR multiplex cinema inside Brookefields screens the latest movies in multiple languages, offering a world-class movie-watching experience with plush seating and Dolby Atmos sound. Kids and teenagers love the Fun City gaming zone, which has arcade games, VR experiences, and interactive rides.
The food court inside Brookefields offers over 25+ dining options, ranging from global fast-food chains like McDonald’s, KFC, and Pizza Hut to authentic South Indian restaurants like Annapoorna and Sree Krishna Sweets. Coffee lovers can enjoy beverages at Starbucks, Café Coffee Day, or Barista, while those with a sweet tooth can explore bakeries like Krispy Kreme and The Donut Company.
The mall also features an exclusive parking space with a capacity of 1,000+ vehicles, centralized air-conditioning, and well-maintained interiors, ensuring a comfortable shopping experience. Brookefields is also known for hosting festive events, live music performances, fashion shows, and seasonal sales, making it a dynamic shopping hub that never loses its charm.

Fun Republic Mall

Fun Republic Mall, located in Peelamedu, is another premier shopping destination in Coimbatore, catering to both luxury and budget shoppers. This 150,000 square feet shopping complex houses a mix of high-end fashion brands, entertainment facilities, and food joints, making it a preferred weekend getaway. The mall boasts an impressive selection of branded stores, including Allen Solly, Van Heusen, UCB, and Puma, attracting fashion-conscious shoppers. Unlike Brookefields, Fun Republic has a more compact yet stylish layout, making navigation easier. A major attraction here is the PVR Cinemas, which screens the latest blockbuster movies in 4K projection with Dolby surround sound. With multiple screens, reclining seats, and luxury VIP lounges, it offers a premium movie-going experience. The mall’s gaming zone is a hotspot for youngsters, featuring arcade games, bowling alleys, and 3D simulation rides. Special gaming tournaments are also hosted, making it a paradise for gaming enthusiasts. When it comes to food, Fun Republic Mall offers popular eateries like KFC, Subway, Domino’s, and Baskin Robbins. Fine dining options include Sigree Global Grill for barbecue and grilled delicacies. The food court also has an exclusive street food section serving North and South Indian chaat items. Another highlight of Fun Republic Mall is its seasonal discount sales, fashion exhibitions, and pop-up stores, where visitors can find great deals on clothing, electronics, and home decor. With its modern interiors, ample parking space, and easy accessibility, Fun Republic Mall is a go-to spot for fashion lovers, moviegoers, and foodies in Coimbatore.

Lakshmi Complex

Lakshmi Complex, located in Town Hall, is Coimbatore’s top electronics and gadget shopping center, offering everything from mobile phones, laptops, and smart gadgets to CCTV cameras and gaming consoles. This commercial complex has over 100 electronic stores, making it the go-to place for students, tech enthusiasts, and business professionals looking for budget-friendly gadgets. Brands like Samsung, Apple, Lenovo, Dell, and HP have authorized retailers here, ensuring genuine products at reasonable prices. Apart from new gadgets, Lakshmi Complex also houses repair centers and service stations for mobile phones and computers. Whether you need a screen replacement, motherboard repair, or software installation, skilled technicians here offer quick and affordable solutions. Shoppers can also find affordable second-hand mobile phones and refurbished laptops, making it a great place for budget-conscious buyers. Some stores even offer custom-built gaming PCs and accessories, attracting gaming enthusiasts. With reasonable prices, a huge variety of tech products, and expert repair services, Lakshmi Complex remains the most popular electronics shopping hub in Coimbatore.

Shree Devi Textile

Shree Devi Textile, located in Gandhipuram, is Coimbatore’s most reputed saree showroom, offering a vast collection of Kanchipuram silk sarees, Banarasi sarees, bridal wear, and party wear sarees. With a legacy of over 50 years, this textile store attracts thousands of customers from Tamil Nadu, Kerala, and Karnataka. The showroom is famous for its authentic silk and cotton sarees, available in traditional motifs, handwoven patterns, and vibrant colors. Brides and wedding shoppers visit this store for its exclusive bridal saree collections, featuring heavy embroidery, zari work, and intricate threadwork. Apart from sarees, the showroom also offers a wide range of men's ethnic wear, including sherwanis, designer kurtas, dhotis, and wedding suits. The fabric collection includes pure silk, georgette, crepe, and velvet materials, suitable for festive and special occasions. Shree Devi Textile is well-known for its customer service, where expert staff members assist shoppers in selecting the best designs based on their preferences. The store also offers customized blouse stitching services to match the sarees purchased. Frequent festive discounts, seasonal sales, and loyalty programs make Shree Devi Textile a favorite shopping destination for traditional and ethnic wear lovers in Coimbatore.

Oppanakara Street

Oppanakara Street is Coimbatore’s busiest shopping hub, known for its wholesale textile markets, budget-friendly jewelry stores, and household goods outlets. This historical shopping street has been a trade center for over 100 years, attracting both local buyers and business owners. The street is lined with over 500 shops, selling cotton sarees, silk fabrics, dress materials, readymade garments, and ethnic wear at wholesale prices. Many shopkeepers source textiles directly from handloom manufacturers, ensuring authentic quality at lower prices. Apart from textiles, Oppanakara Street is also home to gold and silver jewelry stores, where shoppers can find traditional Tamil-style ornaments, temple jewelry, and modern designs at competitive rates. The street also has shops specializing in footwear, handbags, home appliances, and decorative items. Bargaining is an essential skill here, as shopkeepers expect customers to negotiate prices. Street vendors selling hot samosas, fruit juices, and local snacks make shopping here even more enjoyable. If you love street shopping and exploring local markets, Oppanakara Street is a must-visit destination in Coimbatore.
