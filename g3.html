Ooty, also known as Udhagamandalam, is a stunning hill station about 85 km from Coimbatore. Known as the Queen of Hill Stations, Ooty is part of the Nilgiri Hills, famous for its cool climate, tea plantations, and scenic landscapes. The journey from Coimbatore to Ooty via Coonoor and Mettupalayam offers breathtaking views of mist-covered hills, dense forests, waterfalls, and sprawling tea estates.
