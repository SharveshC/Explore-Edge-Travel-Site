Popular Temples in Coimbatore

Discover sacred temples and spiritual experiences
Famous Ganesha Temple

Eachanari Vinayagar Temple

Home to one of the largest Ganesha statues, this temple is known for its spiritual significance and peaceful surroundings.

Hill Temple

Maruthamalai Murugan Temple

Located on a scenic hilltop, this temple offers panoramic views and is dedicated to Lord Muruga.

Ancient Temple

Perur Pateeswarar Temple

A historic temple with exquisite Dravidian architecture dating back several centuries.

City Temple

Koniamman Temple

A popular goddess temple in the heart of the city known for its annual car festival.

Historic Site

Kottai Eswaran Temple

One of the oldest temples in Coimbatore with historical significance and beautiful stone carvings.

Mountain Temple

Velliangiri Andavar Temple

Known as the 'Kailash of the South', this temple requires a trek through scenic hills to reach the summit.
