Kovai Kitchen, located inside Fairfield by Marriott, Coimbatore, is a renowned fine-dining restaurant offering an exquisite blend of traditional Tamil Nadu cuisine and contemporary flavors. Designed with elegant interiors and a warm ambiance, it provides an inviting atmosphere for business travelers, families, and food lovers.

The restaurant is widely known for its lavish breakfast buffet, featuring a live dosa counter, soft idlis, crispy vadas, pongal, puri masala, and freshly brewed Kumbakonam Degree Coffee, making it a must-visit for those who love authentic South Indian flavors. Signature dishes include Kovai-style biryani, Kari dosa, Chettinad chicken curry, and mutton sukka, along with fresh seafood delicacies like prawn thokku, crab masala, and fish curry. The vegetarian offerings are equally impressive, featuring Vazhaipoo Vadai, Kathirikai Kara Kuzhambu, and podi-coated idlis. Complementing the meal are refreshing beverages like Madurai-style Jigarthanda and a range of traditional sweets such as Elaneer Payasam, Mysore Pak, and creamy payasams.
